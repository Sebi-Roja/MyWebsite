Ridable Golf Bag

Tools SolidWorks, — add more —
Deliverable Prototype / CAD model

Overview

For a final Mechanical Engineering Design project, I was assigned a group that was tasked with finding a problem and designing a solution. The agreed upon project was a rideable golf bag to help physically limited people traverse a golf course.

Design & engineering

After trial and error sketching models and theorizing the design's behavior, the time came to produce a prototype. I, along with two members of our group, went to a hardware store to gather wooden supports, tape, cardboard, wheels, and screws to assemble our prototype. The two others assembled the frame while I assembled an Arduino, motor, and potentiometer circuit to power the motor and respond to the amount of current flowing, in order to mitigate the number of revolutions of the tires from the steering bar.
